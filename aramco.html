Compliance with Aramco Engineering Standards

Overview

Our engineering and technical services are designed to meet or exceed the requirements defined in Saudi Aramco Engineering Standards (SAES). This includes but is not limited to standards such as:

  • SAES-A-112: Design Criteria
  • SAES-B-064: Welding Requirements
  • SAES-K-005: Instrument Installation
  • SAES-J-902: Telecommunications Requirements
